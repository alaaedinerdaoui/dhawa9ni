# **App Name**: Haya Tunis Pastries

## Core Features:

- Welcome & Food Showcase: Display a prominent hero section with a brand description, an 'Order Now' call to action leading to the Commander page, and an appetizing gallery of pastry photos with names, descriptions, and prices.
- Product Catalog (Commander): Present a full catalog of Tunisian pastries in a responsive grid layout, with each item showing an image, name, price, quantity selector, and 'Add to Cart' functionality. Product data will be fetched from Firestore.
- Shopping Cart (Panier): Allow users to review selected items, view quantities and individual prices, see the total price, adjust item quantities, remove individual items, or clear the entire cart. Cart data will be stored and managed using Firestore.
- Order Checkout: Provide a clear 'Checkout' button on the cart page to initiate the purchasing process, indicating readiness to finalize an order.
- Pastry Product Description Tool: An AI-powered internal tool for generating compelling and appetizing product descriptions for traditional Tunisian pastries, helping brand owners craft enticing content for the website.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A rich, deep ochre-gold (#8C5E0A) conveying warmth, tradition, and a premium, appetizing feel, reminiscent of toasted nuts and golden pastries. This color grounds the brand's aesthetic and provides a strong, inviting base.
- Background color: A very subtle, warm beige (#F9F5EE) serving as a clean and elegant canvas. Its soft luminosity complements the primary color, allowing product photography to stand out vibrantly.
- Accent color: A vibrant, earthy burnt orange (#DB662B) chosen for its inviting energy and effective contrast against the primary and background hues. It is used to draw attention to key interactive elements like 'Order Now' and 'Add to Cart' buttons, adding an appetizing visual pop.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end feel that captures the premium essence of traditional pastries. Body font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for clean readability and a modern touch, ensuring comfort across all textual content.
- Use clean, minimalistic outline icons that maintain the modern UI aesthetic. Icons for cart actions (add, remove), quantity adjustment, and general navigation should be clear and understated.
- The layout emphasizes a grand hero section on the homepage and clean, responsive grid systems for product displays and cart contents, ensuring optimal viewing across both mobile and desktop devices. Elements are spaced generously to convey a sense of premium quality and ease of use.
- Implement smooth and subtle animations for transitions between sections, hover effects on interactive elements like buttons and product cards, and 'Add to Cart' feedback, enhancing the user experience without distracting from the content.
